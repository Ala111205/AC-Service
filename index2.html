Residential

Spilt Hi-Wall Air Condioner

A high wall split system is generally referred to as a split system and utilises air movement to facilitate comfort. Mounted on a high wall, it has similar qualities to an evaporative cooling system and is generally a preferred air conditioning system for homeowners with closed-off living spaces.

Air Purifier

An air purifier is an electric appliance that removes pollutants and other fine particles from the surrounding air.

Commercial

Cassette Air Condioner

Cassette or ceiling-mounted ACs are an expensive but effective solution for cooling large spaces A cassette air conditioner (AC) is a type of split AC where the fan and air distribution unit is mounted on the ceiling while the compressor is mounted on the roof or elsewhere on the building.

Ducted Air Condioner

Ducted air conditioning is a central air conditioning system that uses discrete ducting to deliver uniform heating and cooling throughout every room of the house.

VRV Air Condioner

VRV or Variable Refrigerant Volume is a state-of-the-art energy-efficient air conditioning system that varies the flow of refrigerant based on your requirements.

Floor Standing Air Condioner

Floor-standing air conditioners are cooling appliances that are typically mounted (or positioned) on or near the floor.

Roof Top

A rooftop air conditioner, also known as a packaged rooftop unit, is a self-contained HVAC system installed on a building's roof. These units differ from split systems in that they combine the condenser and air handler into a single package.

Industrial

Chillers

A chiller is an essential component of HVAC systems that provides cooling by removing heat from water or air. It is used in commercial, industrial, and large-scale residential applications.

Protable Air Condioner

A portable air conditioner is a type of AC that can be easily moved around the house. Also known as a stand-up air conditioner, it is a self-contained unit that can be set up anywhere next to a window and a socket.

Industrial Refrigeration

An industrial air conditioning system is designed to control the temperature, humidity, and air quality in large industrial spaces. Industrial systems are built to handle larger loads.

Stationary Refrigeration

Comfort air conditioning heating applications include a wide range of systems, from small room air conditioners to unitary systems and heat pumps, serving the cooling and heating requirements from residential needs through large commercial applications.
